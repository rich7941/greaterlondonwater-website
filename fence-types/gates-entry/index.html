Commercial Security Gates for Industrial Properties

At Dollar Fence, we specialise in high-performance commercial security gates designed to safeguard your property while streamlining access for authorised personnel. Built for durability and reliability, our commercial gate systems are perfect for warehouses, distribution centres, factories, office complexes, government buildings, apartment communities, and other commercial or industrial sites.

Whether you're looking to improve perimeter security, control traffic flow, or upgrade your facility's professional appearance, our custom-built commercial gates deliver long-term protection with minimal maintenance.

Heavy-Duty Gates Engineered for Commercial Use

Our commercial gates are built using high-grade steel or aluminium to ensure structural integrity, impact resistance, and long-term performance under heavy daily use. The dual-swing gate design showcases vertical pickets and spear tops for an added layer of security and deterrence. This type of gate provides clear visibility, deters climbing, and conveys a secure, professional presence for your property.

Each commercial gate system is custom-engineered to meet your exact specifications, including width, height, operational style, and security needs. Whether you're managing traffic to a loading dock or securing an access point at an apartment complex, we build gates that perform flawlessly and look sharp doing it.

Automated Gate Systems with Access Control

Dollar Fence offers a comprehensive range of automated gate systems designed to meet your specific security and access control needs. From slide gates perfect for tight spaces to sophisticated access control integration, our gate systems provide reliable, secure, and convenient entry solutions for commercial and residential properties.

Built for Security, Designed for Peace of Mind

Security is critical for commercial properties, and our gate systems are designed to provide reliable access control and perimeter protection. The gates are fabricated with anti-climb features, tamper-proof hardware, and reinforced frames that withstand pressure, impact, and attempted breaches. Combined with our fencing options, Dollar Fence offers a full security solution for properties that demand control and confidence.

Slide Gates

Perfect for tight spaces or parallel travel. Smooth operation with space-efficient design for properties with limited clearance areas.

Swing Gates

Ideal for wider openings and traditional aesthetics. Dual-swing or single-swing options available for various access requirements.

Barrier Arms

High-traffic commercial or parking operations. Fast operation with vehicle detection and access control integration.

Vertical Pivot Gates

Compact properties and fast operation. Lift gates for areas requiring quick access with minimal space requirements.

Access Control Integration

Keypad, RFID badge reader, remote control, phone app, or vehicle sensors. Complete integration with existing security systems.

Safety Features

Entry Logging
