Trex Fence Systems

Since launching in 2005, Trex has become the go-to choice for homeowners seeking a private outdoor space framed by a stylish and maintenance-free fence. Built by the world's top manufacturer of outdoor living materials, Trex fences deliver beauty and durability in one seamless system.

Every fence starts with strength—and Trex posts are made of dense wood-plastic composite (WPC) with thick walls capable of standing up to high winds. Posts are anchored into concrete footings and typically require no additional reinforcement (except for gates or extra-tall fences). With height options exceeding 6 feet, we also offer 12-foot posts to ensure deep, secure installations.

Trex Fence Care

Installation Guidelines

Follow proper installation guidelines. Avoid placing your Trex fence near high heat sources such as fire pits, grills, or glass reflections.

Heat Management

Darker Trex colors like Golden Oak or Red Chestnut may warm up in direct sun—use awnings or shade structures to mitigate heat retention.

Cleaning

Clean dirt with mild soap and water. Always test cleaning agents on a hidden section before full application.

Important Warnings

Do not paint Trex fencing. Products like bug spray or sunscreen can cause discoloration if sprayed directly. Apply these away from the fence. Avoid rubber mats near the base, as they may cause surface discoloration.
